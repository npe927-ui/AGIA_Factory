Wake Up Writing

Q: When is the best time to begin writing?

A: As soon as you get up. Get a thought, write a thought. Have no thoughts - write about those non- thoughts. Got nothing to say - write about how you have nothing to say. Can't see it - write about what you can't see. Don't feel anything - then write about what you don't feel. All in all - it's no big deal. To write - you write.
