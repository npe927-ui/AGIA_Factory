# Walking for Dollars

**De:** Matt Furey <matt@mattfurey.com>
**Fecha:** Tue, 10 Mar 2026 14:54:24 +0000
**ID:** 19cd83df3c6f6a15

The idea is nothing new. It's been practiced for ages, even before capitalism was invented.

You're stuck in your idea room, so you go for a walk to ruminate, to expand the room in your mind.

While walking the ideas come to you from "the field."

Center field, right field, left field.

The ideas come to you from above your head and from below your feet. Some of the ideas are in front of you, others are catching up from behind.

Next thing you know you're swimming inside an idea, or a bunch of them. Ideas are buzzing like bees inside your brain.

As you're walking you stop from time to time to record what comes to you, then you return to the confines of your idea room and begin to flesh them out.

You put flesh and bones on your ideas.

And then you put some meat on the hooks - the hooks you will use to reel some real customers in, merging what you know with what they want.

This is one of many ways to walk yourself rich. Instead of the old way of dialing for dollars, you walk for them in the form of ideas.
