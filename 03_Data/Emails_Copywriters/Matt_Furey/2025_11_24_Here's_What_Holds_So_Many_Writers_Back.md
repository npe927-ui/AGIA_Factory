Here's What Holds So Many Writers Back

If I wanted, I could make a long list of what holds so many writers, email copiers and content creators back.

But if I wanted to hit the bullseye, saving you time and aggravation, I would narrow it down to two things:
1. The fear of flopping and...
2. The fear of being embarrassed and humiliated after flopping

Now, the dirty little secret that so many aspiring "great ones" don't realize is...

The great ones flopped and kept on moving. The great ones flopped their way to the top.

More importantly, the difference between those who flop and keep moving - and those who flop and give up - is a matter of conditioning.

If you are conditioned to give up at the first sign of failure, how are you ever going to succeed?

There's only one way out of this trap. You have to recondition yourself until you automatically act in a different way.

As I tell my clients, "The road to success is not paved with perfect scorecards."
