I Dare You to Write a Sentence Like This

This assignment is not what you think it is. And it's super simple, unless you psychologize and make it difficult.

Grab a pen and write the following sentence:

"I don't need to be a great writer to write great."

If you can do this, you may be on your way to greatness.

Why?

Because great writers don't think about whether they are great or not. Their focus is on writing something and making IT great.

Here endeth the lesson.
