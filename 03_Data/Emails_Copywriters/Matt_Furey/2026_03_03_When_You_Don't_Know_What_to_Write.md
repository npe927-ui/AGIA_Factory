Dear Matt,
When I sit down to write I can't come up with anything but nonsense and "I don't know." I'm stuck. Suggestions?

Plior Treyde

Dear Plior,
I don't know what to write either, so I am beginning with "I don't know." Those are the first words I wrote today and for some 'magical' reason more words came after that. The words I wrote may or may not be pure nonsense, but I'll never know if I don't move my pen across the page. At bare minimum I have words I can look at and if need be, edit. When ideas are all in your head, when you think your thoughts are nothing but nonsense, write the nonsense anyway and turn it into uncommon cents.
