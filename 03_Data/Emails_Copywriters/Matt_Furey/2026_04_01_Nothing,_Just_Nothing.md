Nothing, Just Nothing

There's really nothing to write about today. There's nothing going on in the world. Nothing is new. Nothing is exciting. Nothing gets my attention. So what am I supposed to write about? NOTHING?

YES!

Nothing is EVERYTHING.

Nothing is where everything begins. So when you start with nothing, and write anything, you end up with something.

And that something can truly turn into something.

Whoever said there's no such thing in the world as something for nothing got it twisted. The truth is there's no such thing in the world as something that didn't come from nothing.

If you'll write about nothing today - I'm betting it'll be something.
