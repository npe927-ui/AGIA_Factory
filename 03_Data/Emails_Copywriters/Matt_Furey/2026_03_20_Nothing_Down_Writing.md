Nothing Down Writing

There are more than a couple ways to look at the subject of today's email. I'll focus on two.

The first, you get all your writing knowledge with zero up front investment. You pay nothing to anyone and figure you will figure it all out on your lonesome. Good luck!

The second, you want to be a writer but... you rarely and perhaps never, put words down on paper. How you ever going to become good at something you never or seldom do? 'Splain that one to me.

As Dean F.V. DuVall taught so long ago, "To write - you write."

Sure, there's more tricks to this game, and it is a game as well as a profession, than the four words shown above - but none of those tricks work if you don't write.

Here endeth the lesson.
