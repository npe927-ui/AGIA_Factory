Before I made my first million, I was the most educated broke person you've ever met.

I read everything. Attended the seminars. Highlighted the passages. I could quote wealth principles the way some people quote
sports stats.

And I was broke.

Not a little broke. Completely, desperately, can't-make-rent broke — with a bookshelf that could pass for a small library.

If this is ringing a bell, stay with me.

There's a difference between information and installation.

Information lives in your head. You can talk about it at dinner. Nod along when someone brings it up.

Installation is when it gets into your operating system. When it changes what you actually do on a Tuesday afternoon when nobody's
watching.

Think about swimming. You can memorize every stroke, every breathing technique, every turn. But the first time you jump in the
pool, your body doesn't know any of that. Your body only knows what it's experienced.

Your financial blueprint works the same way.

If the program installed in you before age ten says "money is hard to come by" or "people like us don't get rich" — that program
is running your decisions, whether you agree with it or not.

That's exactly what happened to me. I understood wealth principles intellectually. But my blueprint was set for broke. Every time
I gained momentum, the thermostat kicked in and pulled me right back down.

The shift happened when I stopped trying to learn my way to wealth and started doing the actual reprogramming work.

That's when I went from zero to millionaire in 2.5 years.

So here's the takeaway.

Stop measuring your progress by what you know. Start measuring it by what's actually changed in your behavior.

If the knowledge and the bank account don't match, it's not a matter of willpower. It's an installation problem. And that's good
news because installation is solvable.

For 25 years, the thing that actually moved the blueprint was a room. Me on stage. Real questions that got past your first answer
and we ended up eventually reaching the belief underneath.

I've built that into a single mentor session you can do online.
