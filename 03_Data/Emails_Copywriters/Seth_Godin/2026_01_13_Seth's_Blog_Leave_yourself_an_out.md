“Leave yourself an out” [ https://p.feedblitz.com/r3.asp?l=527544081&f=1081591&c=26653669&u=341332914 ]

This is the first rule of safe driving. Don't hurtle your car into a jam where you have no options.

But the first rule of management and human interaction is to leave other people an out.

When you give people a chance to take action that helps them get to where they're going, they'll often seek to go there. • Email to a friend •
