The right sort of friction [ https://p.feedblitz.com/r3.asp?l=542488557&f=1081591&c=29001373&u=341332914 ]

If we remove impediments that are in the way of where our customers seek to go, they support us.

But when we remove the friction that gives people traction on their journey, they flounder.

Remove the hassles that people don't care about, but celebrate the hassles that make it worth the effort. • Email to a friend •
