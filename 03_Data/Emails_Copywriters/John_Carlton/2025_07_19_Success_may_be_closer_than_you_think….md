Even a small list can work…Howdy.

What’s nice about today’s marketing world is you don’t have to rely on big companies or publishers to make your mark. 

Thus, achieving a level of success that once seemed hopeless has become completely feasible. 

I would have re-focused my energy ten-fold when I got started if I knew I could publish my stuff at will. 

As a kid, I enjoyed writing short science fiction stories and graphic novels of cartoons.

Immature stuff, but edgy and entertaining.

My audience consisted solely of my pals, a few teachers who caught me drawing during class, and the occasional stranger.

Back then, I didn’t care because I wasn’t writing for an audience. 

Telling my stories was a thrill in and of itself.

The thought of getting anything published wouldn’t have tickled even my wildest fantasy. 

Then I met Gary Halbert and discovered the art of self-publishing. 

The field is now wide open. And I gotta tell you…

... it’s freaking exhilarating to write and get paid for your own stuff. 

Even a small audience can support a freelance, consulting, or  speaking career.

No, it’s not easy…
