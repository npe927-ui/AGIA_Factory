Simplest tip yet for copywriters... Howdy. 

You wanna be a "real" writer in this age of chatbots?

Then here’s a stellar tip for you: 

Write.

Get ChatGPT to spew out a first draft if you must. 

It could save you some time. (Actually, it’s pretty damn cool).

But don’t leave it at that. 

Dive into it, change it up, add YOUR (or your client’s) personality. 

Because if I haven’t mentioned it before…

… real writers write.

Then they rewrite.

They study language and persuasion and communication…

… after all that, they rewrite some more while putting the new skills to work immediately (constantly jettisoning the bad tactics and exercising the good ones).

Have this tattooed on your amygdala:

Writers write.

This requires time and solitary work. 

Write.

Write, write, write.

Keep journals, exchange emails with other writers, always have a book cooking, read good writers and study their technique, and write well every time you craft a sentence.

Pen emails that others actually print out and keep.

Keep notepads nearby at all times, and wear your ink-stained shirts with pride.

And rewrite everything before you let it out into the world.

Edit, sculpt, and fortify each piece you write.

The scribe guild was one of the first to manifest after civilization formed. Your writing skills can change the world…

... or bore folks to tears. 

Master the craft.

Real writers write.

Heck, you should even make your To Do Lists sizzle with good verb choices.
