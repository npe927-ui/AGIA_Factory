This productivity zapper will get you back in motion…Howdy.

What better way to kickstart the weekend than with a super quick productivity hack to set you up for a killer week ahead?

Sure, it’s the middle of February, and maybe all you want to do is huddle inside under a fuzzy blanket and not change out of your pajamas until July.

And a little hibernation isn’t necessarily a bad thing. 

Some downtime is needed to recharge your batteries. 

But when it’s time to get back into the swing of things, here’s something that helped boost me to the upper echelons of success.

Ridiculously simple, too:

I just trick myself into working. 

I love this tactic and I’ve done it for decades. (You’d think I would be wise to my own evil plans by now.)

But no, I can fool myself anytime.

Whenever faced with a daunting task, I promise myself I’m just gonna take a quick little peek at it. 

That’s all. 

Just one eensy weensy look before slacking off the rest of my day.

All I have to do is just read one page, one email, one video, whatever…

And I like reading, so it’s not even work, really.

And once I start reading, it’s really nothing to jot down a sentence or two in a notebook.

Pretty soon, I have a few pages of brainstorms and research and I barely had to think about it. 

My lazy-ass brain falls for it every freaking time. 

Focusing on one small thing fires up the entire engine, and once started, it feels pretty effortless to just keep going.

I am so gullible to my own tricks.

It’s embarrassing, really.

Now go write just one little word or two. 

It’ll only take a second.
