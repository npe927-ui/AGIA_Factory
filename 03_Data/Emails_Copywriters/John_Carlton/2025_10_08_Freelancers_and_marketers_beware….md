Dudes like this exist, seriously. Here’s how to bypass them… Howdy.

Here’s your shockingly true fact of the day:

Clients are weird, sometimes. 

Okay, maybe that’s not a total shocker.

EVERY freelancer in every field has a story. (Usually a ton, in fact.) 

I swear one of my first clients lost sleep at night praying for my copy to fail miserably…

… even though he was shelling out big bucks for me to write it in the first place. 

Humans really are a perverse species sometimes, I tell ya. 

Greed is one of the most powerful motivators…

… but nothing compares to ego. 

And I guess this dude loved the feeling of lording it over other people even more than he loved money…

… smug, sadistic, self-assured nincompoop that he was. 

All kinds of folks out there in the world. 

If you’re gonna put yourself in the fray, there’s a good chance you’ll encounter azzholes like this dude at some point. 

Fortunately for me, I had a secret to reaching my dreams in spite of sociopaths, and now I’ll share it with you.

It’s simple, too:

Effective brainstorming. 

Coming up with a slew of ideas…

… and then running them through a merciless gauntlet.

And I do mean merciless.

There are ZERO points for "nice try." 

Sometimes I’d get savagely destroyed.

But I was okay with that, it’s all in the process. 

Brainstorming–with zero negativity– is just plain wrong. 

You’ll come up with a few dumb ideas, and that’s okay… 

… as long as you recognize them for what they’re worth and jettison them for something better. 

The cruel, cold hard truth can feel soul crushing, but it is necessary.

Remember prospects ONLY buy your product if it’s what they want. 

Not because it’s cute or clever or your ad made them laugh. 

This confusion is RAMPANT among even veteran business owners.

That’s also why effective and merciless brainstorming is essential to success. 

Getting your ass whooped from time to time is a good thing if it leads to better ideas in the end.

Stay frosty,
