Howdy.

Want to be a successful marketer, richer than your wildest dreams of avarice?

Well here’s a strange and oddly effective way to do it:

You gotta get deeply in touch with your own emotions.

Yes, you read that right. 

No matter what we love to tell ourselves, we all buy for emotional reasons. 

You. 

Me. 

Your auto mechanic…

… your accountant.

Everyone. 

Smart marketers don’t deny this fact. 

So all the good writers I know are drenched with self-knowledge and empathy for the emotional experiences of others. 

That’s not to say you’ll catch us walking around sobbing hysterically… 

… but we are easily overcome with the feeling of a situation.

I often have tears in my eyes as I finish writing something because I get so deep into the tub of emotional goo I’ve created.

And this extra dose of REAL emotion is no accident. 

You cannot be a good writer or marketer without empathy.

You have to feel everything humans are capable of feeling.

At full strength, too. 

The industrial-quality stuff.

The intensity of your ability to feel infuses your writing with power.

But if your emotions are in lock-down…

… you will always struggle to be able to get into another person’s head.

Finding that sweet spot of need and connection is what transforms great sales copy into a license to print money.

You don’t have to become a Drama Queen.

But you do need to embrace your ability to know joy, sadness and yes, even pain. 

Emotions are the building blocks of a well-lived life and of a very, very effective writer.

No one gets out of here without a few tears.

So learn to be a sap already. 

It will help you engage with life more fully, and write with real passion.
