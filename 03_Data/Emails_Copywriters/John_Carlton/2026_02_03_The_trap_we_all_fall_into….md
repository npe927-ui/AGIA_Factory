here’s a way out… Howdy.

Lots of things can put out your fire as a biz owner and marketer. 

Lack of confidence. 

Overconfidence. 

Out of date knowledge. Etc.

But one of the biggest by far...

... is procrastination.

And EVERYONE does it…

… even if they swear they don’t.

Yes, there are emails to write and products and campaigns to develop…

… but first, just 5 MORE MINUTES ON FACEBOOK!

The modern world has more distractions than ever. 

Back in the day, parents used to scold us that our minds would turn to jello if we didn’t turn off the TV. 

These days, folks while away hours to discover what type of potato they are. 

Procrastination really is a monster…

… and there’s no foolproof cure for it…

… because there isn’t just one reason for it. 

It’s right there in our DNA and modern tech is just making things worse. 

And if we can’t cure it, the next best thing is to learn how to deal with it.

Every top professional in every field MUST have a tactic to overcome procrastination. 

Here’s mine: 

Take your final deadline and divide the time you have into work days.

Then, focus like a laser for a short time on just one thing. 

This really engages your brain and pulls you into your work. 

In copy, I usually write up my bullets first.

Then the USP will be easy.

* Once that’s firmly rooted, the headline, opening paragraphs, and the close just sorta burble up on their own.

* Then I’ll move on to the guarantee, the details of the deal, all the good stuff that finishes up the piece. 

* Then, I go back and dink around with the supporting paragraphs — the story, the proof, the testimonials, etc.

I don’t worry about anything except the exact thing I'm working on in that moment. 

A little bit of focus really takes the stress out of the whole process.

This is something you can’t do if you regard each job as a massive single monster. 

But in small bites? 

Then you might start procrastinating, but in a good way. 

Oh, my favorite Hollywood celebrity did what now?

I’ll check it out in 5 more minutes…

… as soon as I’ve finished polishing up this email.

It really works, you know. 

Try it.
