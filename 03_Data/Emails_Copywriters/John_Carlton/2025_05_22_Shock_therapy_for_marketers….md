Wake up those zombies...Howdy. 

Do you want to level up your marketing skills starting right now? 

Remember that good salesmanship requires occasional shock therapy tactics to pry your prospect's mind away from self-destructive tendencies.

Most folks stumble through life believing they've got reality nailed…

... and as adults, they have largely stopped questioning stuff.

Knowing this is an advantage to a marketer.

If you want to wake up those zombies who are your prospects, you need a killer hook that demolishes accepted wisdom or "common sense".

The trick is not to just blindly startle anyone, though. 

All that does is start an argument no one can win.

Instead -- you gotta finesse the hook to jar them out of unconsciousness so they jolt awake and say, "Wait, can that possibly be true?"

Then the real trick begins…

… because now you have to deliver on the promise of the hook. 

Just never underestimate the ability of the human brain to lock up on the dumbest possible options.

You're dealing with the most stubborn and nihilistic species on the planet, dude.
