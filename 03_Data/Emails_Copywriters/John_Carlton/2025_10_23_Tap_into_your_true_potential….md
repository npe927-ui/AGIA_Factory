Reaching the pinnacle of your most ambitious dreams does NOT require genius-level thinking or creativity. 

But you cannot realize your potential without the ability to brainstorm effectively.

Folks naturally suck at it. 

The idea makes them recoil in horror… 

… especially when it dawns on them that "nice try" counts for nothing in the real world.

When you stick your nose into the fray, sometimes you get savagely destroyed.

That’s all just part of the game, because hatching up new ideas can be gruesome…

… particularly in the early stages. 

Think of it like sausage-making.

Everyone loves the final result.

But no one wants a glimpse into the actual slaughterhouse. 

However, entrepreneurs and marketers are a different breed.

So in honor of that gruesome metaphor, here are a couple of sausage-making points to help wiggle good ideas out of your head:

* Sausage-Making Point #1: The way most folks are taught to brainstorm–with zero negativity– is just plain wrong. The cruel, cold hard truth can be soul crushing, but it is necessary.

* Sausage-Making Point #2: People confuse the concept of "creativity" all the time. 

"Creativity" can be anything you want it to be outside of the biz world. 

There, the consequences of being wrong are minimal.

But in biz, prospects won’t buy your product because it’s cute or clever. 

They’ll buy it only if it’s what they want. 

This confusion is RAMPANT among even veteran business owners.

True fact — One of my earliest clients was a smug, sadistic, self-assured nincompoop who was hellbent on making his staff writers miserable. 

I loved getting results with my copy, because I knew he lost sleep at night praying for me to fail. 

Silly bastard.

Anyway, don’t be afraid to get your ass whooped from time to time. 

It’ll all lead to better ideas in the end.
