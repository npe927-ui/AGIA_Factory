A tough gig, but this will help...Howdy.

There are a ton of scammers out there selling pure, uncut crap…

… some are making a boatload of money, both online and offline.

Nothing new about this. 

Chances are, they make up a significant portion of your competition, and they’ll do anything to win. 

It’s tough to compete against them. 

But just remember, your customers deserve better, especially when it’s a list of people you’ve worked hard to build a relationship with. 

That trust can all go out the window in an instant, so guard your reputation carefully.

Scams have existed ever since the first caveman convinced his fellow caveman to buy his old cave for a slab of mastodon meat.

The very next transaction could very well have been a scam.

Like maybe trying to sell a fake cave. 

Or a cave with a bear in it. 

"Oh, didn’t I mention that bear? Sorry ‘bout that. But no, you can’t have your slab of meat back."

The thing is, our capitalistic system allows for financial reward regardless of whether you’re selling quality goods or crap.

That’s why there’s never really been a truly "free" market. 

The moment someone with any power got scammed, laws were written and scofflaws were tossed into newly constructed hoosegows.

Anyway, when you’ve been in biz as long as I have — and I’ve been around a loooooong time — then you start to notice certain things that keep happening.

Like the never-ending parade of predatory "get rich quick" scams.

I’m not gonna name names, cuz it’s irrelevant. 

If it wasn’t this particular magic formula, it’s something else.

I’ve lived through three or four of these cycles. 

Hindsight is 20/20, too…

…it all seems so obviously absurd once the dust settles.

But when you’re caught up in the moment…

… damn. 

With the globalized reach of the online business world, it’s EASY to make a bundle selling shoddy, screwed up goods and services.

Because, again, the product often doesn’t matter. 

The product can be terrible, or it can be revolutionary "change your life forever" in ways you can’t even imagine.
