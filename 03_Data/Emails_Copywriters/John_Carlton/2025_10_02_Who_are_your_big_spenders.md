Are you leaving gobs of moolah on the table?Howdy.

Times are tough out there, and you can’t afford to leave money on the table in your marketing. 

Which is exactly what you’re doing if you ignore your demographics.

Market research can seem boring... 

…until you realize how much more your biz could be raking in just by knowing who your prospects are and what they want. 

Surprisingly, many of your best customers would love to spend MORE money with you. 

So why get in the way of their hearts’ desire?

Consider adding one or two more options to your selection. 

For example:

There are die-hard shoppers who will set up camp outside a store waiting for the doors to open just to take advantage of a 10% off deal.

But that’s only one segment of your customer base - and hopefully a very small one, at that. 

Others (myself included) will happily pay a premium for a little special treatment. 

So do the courtesy of making it easy for your big spenders to spend more. 

Many online marketers have never considered a "platinum level" of service.

They’ve never even thought about raising their prices to see what the market will bear. 

When they finally see the light, they are often astonished at how many people will pay more for a higher level of service and a few extra privileges.

And here’s a fact that may knock your socks off:

Every niche has a certain number of people who put more value on time than on money.

Guys like me are extremely willing to pay extra to save time. 
Sometimes a lot extra.

Think about how your online "sales pitch" comes across to prospects. 

They’re looking for help, for advice, for direction…

… and a bargain is often the last thing on their minds.  

I mean, geez Louise.

Won't someone think of the big spenders?
