Amplify your marketing efforts…Howdy.

If you want to join the ranks of great marketers, you’ll need to look at the world differently from regular civilians. 

It starts with being realistic about everything. 

Bubbly optimists tend to get eaten even as they "think positively" about it, and pessimists don’t fare any better. 

So put away whatever shaded lens you’re observing the world through…

… and start thinking like a street smart sales pro.

Get to know your prospects as they are.

* Not as you wish they were.

* Not as you believe them to be.

Look at the raw reality, minus the spin.

All opinions, common sense, long-held beliefs, even principles and convictions…

… must run the gauntlet of a strict internal and external BS Detector.

This includes both the other guy’s actions and thinking behaviors.

And your own.

You gotta clear your brain of a LOT of nonsense before you can even begin to approach the "truth" of any situation.  

As a human, your default setting is to believe that your thoughts, actions, codes of honor, and beliefs are the real deal.

But if you crave success, you must first… 

… realize that the other person has the SAME default setting.

They’re right, and you’re the emotional and irrational one.  

Just like you were thinking how much of a moron the other person is and how lucky you are to be so righteous and close to the "real" truth.

This gets heavy, quickly.

That’s why you also need to run those instincts (and gut feelings) constantly through your BS Detector, especially when you start out.

Every single one of us is front-loaded with piles of unchallenged assumptions and erroneous notions we mistakenly think of as "common sense."

Basically, you just gotta get over your bad self… 

… and then get past the surface layers of the market you’re in (and all the people populating it).

Above all, get clear on how folks actually behave and act.

Prime example:

When asked, people love to SAY they always buy "quality" over cost. That’s cuz it sounds like the smart thing to do. 

And everyone wants to appear smart. 

But when it comes to opening their wallet…

…they immediately choose bargain-priced crap over the slightly more expensive stuff that’s waaayyyy better.

That, my friend, is just the tip of the iceberg.

When you begin regular brutal reality checks… 

… you are scuba-diving deep into the seldom-explored hidden realities of Humans In The Asphalt Jungle. 

Still, you always need to know what you’re up against.

A realistic world view gives you the best chance of success, at life, in relationships, and in biz.
