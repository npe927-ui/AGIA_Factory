Two choices all pros must make…Howdy.

Before I became known in the marketing world, there was a subtle, yet undeniable shift in my mindset.

It turned out to be the foundation of my entire career.

As a professional, you have two choices:

* You’re either in

* Or you’re out.

Mucking about in the muddy middle just pisses everyone off, yourself included.

So I learned to NOT cop an attitude, mope, or bore anyone with my tales of woe. 

Instead, I committed to doing the best damn job I was capable of, and… very important… 

… I immersed myself in the projects at hand.

By "immersed", I mean I rolled up my sleeves and went to work with all the professional tools in my Bag of Tricks. 

This mental tactic was something I had to learn, early on… 

… because the days of a freelancer and marketer are often filled with emergencies and crises and fire drills where everyone else is running around in a panic.

If you’re gonna do your job well, you must be the adult in that room. 

Calm, in control, mindful of what needs to be done and what CAN be done (given deadlines and other obstacles, like financial or labor shortages).

Then, with clarity and purpose… 

… you DO whatever you can to make the project a success. 

No matter what.

After a few seasons in the front-line trenches, you’ll be able to rely on your own experiences for your sense of calm and control. 

Until then, you’ll just have to trust the veterans who are giving you advice.

And trust me on this: 

Anyone can develop these "adult" tactics.

You just have to learn to commit… and immerse.
