Successful entrepreneurs get that way in large part because they love the PROCESS of learning. 

Most people hate it and avoid it like the plague whenever they can.

That’s why you often see the word “discover” instead of “learn” in marketing materials.

Just one more example of the power of the right word. 

But I digress.

If you want to take your biz to new heights…

… you must dedicate yourself to a lifetime of learning. 

Yes, even the boring stuff. Or especially the boring stuff.

Interesting fact: 

This is also why many of the best marketers are also good at a musical instrument.

They don’t mind reviewing the fundamentals again and again and again to drill them deep into their unconscious. 

And getting to the point of competence takes serious discipline. 

Most people, given the choice, will not go through the physical pain (your fingers bleed when learning guitar) or the mental anguish…

…  because you will fail over and over again on each step.

No one gets it right the first time… 

… or even the first hundred times of learning something as sophisticated as a musical instrument.

But musicians at heart don’t have a choice. 

Nor do the world’s best marketers. 

They’re driven by much more than money or glory.

The desire to master the beast comes from within, and they are compelled to do whatever it takes.

Each new level of proficiency is a reward in and of itself.

Luckily, learning copywriting or marketing isn’t nearly as demanding as an instrument…

… but the same underlying principle applies.

It’s a process. 

And you CAN learn it, and you CAN do it. 

It just takes a little guidance.
