Never write without this…Howdy.

Are your marketing messages falling flat?

There’s a simple fix that separates the rookies from the pros.

It’s all a matter of not having prepared yourself for the task.

Here’s a clue: 

The very first step is…

… RESEARCH.

You learn everything you can about the market you’re writing to.   

* Who the competition is
* What they’re doing right
* What they’re doing wrong
* What’s new
* What’s over and done with
* Where the opportunities are.

The second step: 

Get into the head of your prospect.  

Get to know the emotional, physiological, spiritual and psychological needs, wants and world-view of the folks you’re going to persuade.

Every step in creating a killer marketing message rests on the practical information you get through RESEARCH.  

So, when you’re finally ready to sit down and “write”… 

… you know where you’re going, who you’re going after…

… and what you need to do to accomplish it all.

Inspiration has nothing to do with it.

Writing without preparing is like flying to a strange city and then hopping in a car to drive there…

… without an address, or a GPS, or a map, or even advice on where you’re going.

You’ll freeze up at the first cross-street.  

Do you go left?  Right?  Straight?

Preparing to write means you’ll never sit there staring at the blank screen.

Your research is your address, GPS, and map.

Now go and prosper.
