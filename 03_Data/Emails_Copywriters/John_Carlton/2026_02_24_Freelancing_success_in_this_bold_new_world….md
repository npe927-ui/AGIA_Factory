Robots, we’re coming for you…Howdy.

Throughout my career, I’ve watched fascinated as new tech came and went.

(Yes – I loathe tech – but I’m still fascinated by it.)

Even more so by people’s reaction to it.

Panic and mayhem are the norm, especially with the latest disruptive breakthrough throwing a wrench into everything – the rise of A.I.

But here’s what you need to remember:

Business in the modern world still revolves around other human beings. 

Always has, always will. 

Machines ain’t broken the code on all our foibles, desires, idiosyncrasies, weird habits, delusions/dreams/traumatic memories/personal histories/broken hearts and on and on and on...

… in other words…

… the stuff that makes us human.

You ARE gonna have to adjust, as a copywriter, freelancer, or marketer, however.

Cuz not only is AI not going away, it’s just gonna keep getting smarter. 

It’s already nipping at the heels of rookies by doing SOME of the jobs low-level copywriters used to handle.

But you know who will NEVER worry about being replaced?

A-List writers whose salesmanship has been tested and sharpened in the real world. 

More specifically,  A-List writers who have become highly sought after CONSULTANTS.

At that level, you cement your career so solidly that a bulldozer couldn’t dislodge you.

Nearly every high-level biz owner/marketer/manager NEEDS consultants - and will gladly pay through the nose to land a good one.

Owners of moderate-sized businesses are often hobbled trying to translate a 2-year Business Degree into real-world success… 

… and are desperate for serious input from someone who actually know how leads are generated and sales consummated.

Even CEOs running gazillion-dollar companies are often experienced ONLY in boardroom politics.

They can’t come up with winning ideas or copy to save their lives.

So if you want to be forever untouchable and irreplaceable… 

… then fast-track your way to the top, skip every step in between, and become…

… The Consultant Who Also Can Provide The Copy.

This is what the A-Listers are headed these days. 

They’re SO knowledgeable and SO brimming with insight, gut instinct, and skill…

… that they essentially operate as a partner in every job they take.

These elites are - and will remain - the highest paid freelancers in the world…

… because they are so much more than writers.

This is the level you need to aspire to.

If you have holes in your freelance process, then check out this here blog post https://www.john-carlton.com/2024/06/the-simple-secret-to-an-elite-copywriting-career-which-youll-never-guess/.
