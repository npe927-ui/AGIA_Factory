What your dog already knows…Howdy.

It takes a lot of things to make an entrepreneurial adventure work.

Brains, cojones, good networking skills, a bit of luck, vast amounts of energy and drive…

... and the kind of motivation that kicks you in the butt every morning.

Yes, all of that stuff, and more.

But the PRIMARY thing you need to remember is that wealth is only one small part of living a truly great life. 

If you’re rich and miserable and you’ve zapped away your health in the pursuit of avarice… 

… what’s the point? 

It turns out that Abraham Lincoln was right about one thing:

— you are as happy as you decide to be. 

And real success starts with this: 

You gotta be healthy.

All the moolah and possessions in the world won't do a thing for you, if you're gasping for breath and rotting from the inside.

That’s why the foundation of everything you go after is simply…

... being a good animal.

That means taking time every single day to maintain, nurture and groom your entire body and soul. 

Just like an animal in the wild.

* Learn to breathe deeply. An hourly exhaust check does the trick, but you gotta be diligent.

* Eat fabulous fuel-laden food most of the time. Pizza and burgers are treats, not sustenance.

* Meditate like your entire future depends on it. Every day, for at least twenty minutes, zone out and let your brain float in its own juices. It's a natural way to recharge the battery and flush out toxic thoughts.

* Feed your soul, too. However you want to approach your spiritual side is just fine… but it must be done. You're not just a mass of chemical and mechanical chain reactions. There's an essential life force within you (within all of us) that shrivels when ignored.

* And exercise with gusto and purpose. 

You know who does all of this well?

Your dog.

See how she stretches with pleasure, runs and cavorts with joyous abandon, eats and pisses and sleeps with unabashed bliss.

Be more like your damn dog, and rewards await. 

Okay, you can leave out the more gross stuff she does, but try to maintain the suppleness and grace of her existence.

Yes, we all gotta die at some point.

But in the meantime, chew up some scenery and live large. 

Key word: Live. Like a healthy, gleeful beast...

Just sayin'.

Stay frosty,
