# Breakthrough Advertising Bootcamp — Brian Kurtz & Chris Mason

**Price: $197 (bootcamp only) or $297 (with Breakthrough Advertising book)**

---

## The headline

"Read Your Market's Mind and Create Copy That Makes Your Offer the Only Logical Choice in Just 14 Days"

---

## The central insight

> "The greatest mistake marketers make is trying to create demand."

Successful marketing channels existing desires — it doesn't create new ones.

This is the core principle from Eugene Schwartz's *Breakthrough Advertising*, applied in live coaching format.

---

## Why this bootcamp exists

Brian Kurtz had a 20+ year personal relationship with Eugene Schwartz and served as custodian of his legacy. He positions this bootcamp as the most direct way to apply Schwartz's timeless principles to modern markets.

---

## What you'll learn

- How to discover your market's secret desires (not what they say they want — what actually drives them)
- How to find the strongest desire your market is already feeling
- How to present your offer as the inevitable, logical solution to that desire
- How to sell to prospects completely unaware of your product category
- How to hire and brief effective copywriters
- Strategies for entering markets unfamiliar with you or your competition

---

## Program structure

- Six live calls (up to 90 minutes each)
- Five short assignments (~30 minutes each)
- Worksheets, recordings, and private Facebook group
- Two bonus calls: guest experts on SEO and psychology of persuasion

## Social proof

Multiple testimonials from previous bootcamp attendees praising practical value, overdelivery of content, and immediate business application.

## Sales techniques used in this letter

- **Aspirational headline with timeframe:** "only logical choice in just 14 days" — makes the transformation concrete and fast
- **Reframe of marketing fundamentals:** "don't create demand, channel it" — instantly repositions the reader's worldview
- **Legacy authority:** Schwartz's Breakthrough Advertising is a cult text — association with it grants instant credibility
- **Personal relationship with the source:** Brian Kurtz knew Schwartz personally for 20+ years — not just studied, lived it
- **Dual price options:** $197 vs. $297 with book — anchors the higher price and makes the bootcamp alone feel cheap
- **Live format with structure:** six calls + assignments signals accountability, not just passive consumption
- **Expert guest calls as bonus:** adds perceived value without inflating the core price
