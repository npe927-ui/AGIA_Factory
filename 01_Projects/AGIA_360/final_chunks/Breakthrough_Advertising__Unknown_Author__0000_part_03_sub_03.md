On the Limits to the Images Your
Prospects Will Identify With

I must insert a warning here. Although these identification -
images are immensely powerful when used correctly, there arc
also strict rules and limits to their use. And, if they are used in
correctly, they can be disastrous.

Many campaigns have collapsed because they have asked
their market to identify themselves with an unbelievable image.
For example, the deodorant-soap campaign of several vears ago
that used an elite, society image to sell the mass market. The
men and women who composed the market could not make the
jump between themselves and the characters pictured in the ad.
And they not only refused to believe the suggested identification.
but the disbelief spread to the performance claims themselves.
and killed the sales.

The key to avoiding such mistakes, of course, is the struc
ture of the word, unbelievable. What makes an image--or a claim,

themselves, their lives, their present position in society, or their
