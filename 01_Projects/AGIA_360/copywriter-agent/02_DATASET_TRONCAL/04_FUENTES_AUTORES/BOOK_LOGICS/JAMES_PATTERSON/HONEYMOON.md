# James Patterson: Honeymoon (Technical Logic)

## 1. The Suspicion Hook
- **Rule**: Start with a perfect scenario and slowly introduce a single "Wrong" element.
- **Application**: "Your conversion is at 10%. Perfect, right? Except 40% of those are bots. Here's why."

## 2. High-Frequency Pacing
- **Pattern**: White space is your friend. Short chapters. Short paragraphs.
- **Application**: Mobile-first copy. Never more than 3 lines of text without a break.
