# James Patterson: Along Came a Spider (Technical Logic)

## 1. The Propulsive Hook
- **Rule**: Start with a punch. End with a question.
- **Application**: The first line of every social media post/ad must be a "Stop-the-Scroll" hook.

## 2. Dual Perspective (The Hunter and the Hunted)
- **Rule**: Show the plan of the "Villain" (The Problem) and the reaction of the "Hero" (The Solution) in alternating, fast bursts.
- **Application**: In copy, alternate between the "Pain's POV" (what it's doing to the business) and the "Solution's POV" (how it's counter-attacking).
