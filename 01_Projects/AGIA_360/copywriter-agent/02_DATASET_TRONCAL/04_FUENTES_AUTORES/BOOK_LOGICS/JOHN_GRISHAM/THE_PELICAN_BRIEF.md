# John Grisham: The Pelican Brief (Technical Logic)

## 1. The Dangerous Discovery (The Brief)
- **Rule**: Simple curiosity leading to a massive, systemic problem.
- **Application**: Use "The One Insight" that the industry is hiding from the user.

## 2. Isolation and Information
- **Rule**: The hero has information but no support.
- **Application**: Frame the customer as the "Only one in their company who sees the truth," making the SaaS their only ally.
