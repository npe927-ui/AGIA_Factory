# John Grisham: A Time to Kill (Technical Logic)

## 1. The Final Plea (Emotional Transposition)
- **Pattern**: "Now imagine she is white." (The closing argument tactic).
- **Rule**: Use a "Transpose the Context" scenario to force the reader to see the problem from a new, visceral perspective.
- **Application**: "Imagine this was your child's data. Now look at your current security."

## 2. Moral Urgency
- **Rule**: The deadline isn't a clock; it's a moral failure if the result isn't achieved.
- **Application**: Move the deadline from "Time is running out" to "Your team's potential is wasting away."
