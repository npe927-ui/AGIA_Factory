# Lee Child: Killing Floor (Technical Logic)

## 1. The Staccato Rhythm
- **Rule**: Short sentences. No fluff. Fragmented logic.
- **Application**: In copy, use brief, punchy statements of fact. "He was a big man. He had no luggage. He had a problem." -> "The code is clean. The cloud is ready. The profit is yours."

## 2. Technical Violence (Precision)
- **Rule**: Describe physical conflict with anatomical and mechanical precision.
- **Application**: Describe the "Pain Points" with clinical accuracy. Don't say "It’s hard to manage teams," say "34% of your engineering time is leaking into unlogged Jira tickets."
