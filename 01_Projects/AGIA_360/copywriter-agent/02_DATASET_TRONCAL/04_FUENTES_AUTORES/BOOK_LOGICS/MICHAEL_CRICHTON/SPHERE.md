# Michael Crichton: Sphere (Technical Logic)

## 1. The Abyss (Isolation)
- **Rule**: Place characters at the bottom of the ocean. No help is coming.
- **Application**: Communicate to the business owner: "In the next market shift, you will be on your own. This tech is your only oxygen."

## 2. The Manifestation of Thought
- **Rule**: The technology/entity reflects the user's internal fears back at them.
- **Application**: Use the user's *own data* to prove their fears are justified (and solvable).
