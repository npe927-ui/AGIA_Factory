# Michael Crichton: Jurassic Park (Technical Logic)

## 1. Chaos Theory (The Single Failure Point)
- **Rule**: No matter how complex the system, one small, overlooked variable (e.g., frogs' West African DNA) will cause total systemic collapse.
- **Application**: In copy, identify the "Single Failure Point" in the customer's current manual process and show how it leads to inevitable disaster.

## 2. Technical Exposition (The Ian Malcolm Method)
- **Pattern**: Use diagrams and mathematical logic to explain a narrative threat.
- **Application**: Use charts and data visualizations (in description or real assets) to prove the "Logic" of the urgency.

## 3. The Ethical Warning
- **Rule**: "You were so preoccupied with whether or not you could, you didn't stop to think if you should."
- **Application**: Warn the customer about the "Cost of Improper Scaling" without the SaaS.
