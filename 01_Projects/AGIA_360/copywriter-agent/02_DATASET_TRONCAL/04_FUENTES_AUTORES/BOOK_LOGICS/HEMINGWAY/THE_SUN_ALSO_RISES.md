# Hemingway: The Sun Also Rises (Technical Logic)

## 1. The Power of Omission (Dialogue)
- **Pattern**: Characters speak about mundane things (food, drink, travel) while the real conflict (impotence, loss) is never mentioned but always present.
- **Rule**: Let the subtext do the heavy lifting. Avoid explicit emotional labels.
- **Application**: In copy, talk about the "Tools" and "Execution," while the subtext is "Status" and "Power."

## 2. The Sun Rises Anyway
- **Rule**: Use the environment as a constant, indifferent backdrop to human drama.
- **Application**: Position the SaaS/Product as the "Unchanging Foundation" (Market dynamics change, wars happen, but our engine keeps running).
