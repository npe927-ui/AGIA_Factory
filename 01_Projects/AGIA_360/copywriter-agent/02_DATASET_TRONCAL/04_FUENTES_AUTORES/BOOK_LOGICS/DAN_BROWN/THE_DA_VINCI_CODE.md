# Dan Brown: The Da Vinci Code (Technical Logic)

## 1. The Clock-Driven Urgency (The Tick-Tock)
- **Rule**: Every piece of information must be a key to a door that is about to slam shut.
- **Application**: In copy, present "The Secret" as something that will be lost forever if not acted upon immediately.

## 2. Micro-Hooks (The Cliffhanger)
- **Pattern**: Chapters end with a revelation that changes the context of everything came before.
- **Application**: Every email/section must end with a "Wait, there's more" or a "But here's the problem..."

## 3. The Authority of the Expert
- **Rule**: Character (Langdon) is constantly proving their technical authority through rapid-fire facts.
- **Application**: The AI must sound like a world-class historian/expert of the niche, sprinkling in "Forbidden" or "Hidden" data points.
