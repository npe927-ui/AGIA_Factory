# Dan Brown: Deception Point (Technical Logic)

## 1. Technical Authority (NASA/Delta Force)
- **Rule**: Use hyper-specific technical jargon to build absolute trust.
- **Application**: Use industry-specific acronyms and metrics (LTV, CAC, NPS) with surgical precision.

## 2. The Pivot (The Reveal)
- **Rule**: Halfway through, reveal that the "Solution" was actually a "Problem" in disguise (and vice versa).
- **Application**: "You think your SEO is helping? It's actually killing your conversion. Here's why."
