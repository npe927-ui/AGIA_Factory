# Dan Brown: Angels & Demons (Technical Logic)

## 1. The Antagonistic Contrast (Science vs. Religion)
- **Rule**: Frame the conflict as two equally powerful, opposing forces.
- **Application**: In copy, frame the SaaS solution as the "Bridge" between two massive industry pains (e.g., "Speed vs. Security").

## 2. The Geographic Crucible
- **Rule**: The action is physically constrained (Vatican City). Escape is impossible.
- **Application**: Create a "Market Crucible" (In this specific industry, these are the only rules that matter).
