### Introduction

Cold emailing to prospects is one of the most widely used effective strategies for finding new customers. All successful salespeople, in their repertoire of tools and techniques, will have a set of cold email templates which the test and use with a high degree of success. Cold email is just the first step in attempting to open a conversation with your prospect. Even if some one has a need for your product or offering, it often takes - touches and followups before you get a response. So not only do you need an effective email for your first cold approach, you also need to have effective templates to followup and give them more reasons to start a conversation with you. ConversionSpree brings to you the ultimate collection of cold email templates that you can use to create a winning cold email campaign. We have collected over + successful email templates that you can use from first touch to multiple follow ups. Use these templates provided as an inspiration. More importantly, edit them, adapt them and make them more relevant for your own offering.

## Cold Email Templates

This cold email templates is divided into various types as detailed: First Touch Templates: Use these templates when sending the first cold email to your prospect. . Referral Templates: When you write to some one higher up in the prospect organization and request an introduction to the right person . Direct Sales Outreach Templates: Write directly to the appropriate person in your target company and engage in a sales conversation . Value Add Outreach Templates: Write directly to the appropriate person in your target company, to share some thing of value and build a relationship Followup Templates: Use these templates to followup at scale. . Checking in: Remind the prospect gently that you have written to them earlier and you are awaiting a reply . Adding Value: Add value to your prospect, and in the process - remind them of your commercial intent . Use Humor: Add some humor to make your followup email stand out Breakup Email Templates: Typically the last touch in your outreach

### Referral: Who is the right person?

This is an example of referral template that is pointed and direct. This template uses the subject line to do all the work it is not salesy and does not attempt to convince the recipient on the benefits of your offering/ product etc. The subject line is used to providing context. This is a simple and direct template that goes straight to the point. It does not waste time with personal introductions or pleasantries. ## Follow-up Templates

Following up is the most important lever to improve the response rate of your cold email campaigns. Many studies have shown that it takes an average of five follow ups to close a sale. So do not stop after a couple of follow ups; keep following up till you get a response.

Usually, you are banking on the pseudo-psychological manipulation also known as scarcity effect - that people want what they can't have. Our experience has been that "Breakup emails" they tend to work more often than not. Be sure to try them out in one of your cadences and measure the impact.

The mail without wasting any time just lists options and requests the prospect to choose one Leave or Talk later or Talk now. Subject: Should I stay or should I go? Hi { FirstName}}, I know you're busy. Just give me a 'x' or 'x' . We'll pass on partnering with [company] this year, thanks for the offer! . We're interested in the [product], but it's not a good time, reach back out to me in month. . I'm interested let's talk! Thanks! Source

Let me know if this is still an opportunity that you are interested in pursuing? If not, I completely understand and wish you all the best. Regards, XYZ

### Salesy Cold Email Template

Intentionally placed on this last page, it is not encouraged to sell in cold emails to avoid low conversion rates; there is a huge consensus that cold emails convert better for "introduction".
